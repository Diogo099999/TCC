Emergency Resources

are some emergency contacts in Brazil related to valuing life and psychological support:

    CVV (Life Appreciation Center):

    Phone: 188 (available 24/7)

    Website: CCV.ORG

    Psychological Care Center (CAPS):

    Available in several cities and regions. You can find your nearest CAPS by contacting your local Health Department.

    Disque 100

    Phone: 100

    for complaints of human rights violations and protection of children and adolescents, available 24 hours a day

    SAMU (Mobile Emergency Care Service)

    Phone: 192

    (medical emergencies and critical situations, available 24 hours a day)

    Military police

    Phone: 190

    Brazil

    (for emergency situations involving security and crime, available 24 hours a day)

    Remembering that it is important to seek professional help if you or someone you know is experiencing emotional or psychological difficulties. Prevention and support are fundamental for valuing life.
